About Us

Computer Programming Society

The name of this organization shall be Computer Programming Society of the College of Engineering and Computer Studies at Lyceum of the Philippines University-Laguna.

Mission

The Computer Programming Society aims to be an excellent organization in the field of computing to all the students of the College of Engineering and Computer Studies.

Vision

The Computer Programming Society envision itself to create an open environment for the College of Engineering and Computer Studies students to engage in various trends in computing and use their skills competently as they learn. This vision is executed through the following objectives:

1. Create further awareness and understanding of the different trends in computer programming, not limited to web and mobile development,

2. Participate in various competitions held inside and outside the campus,

3. Help the students to retain their chosen program at the College of Engineering and Computer Studies,

4. Foster social interaction, collaboration and camaraderie about programming among student members,

5. Develop a high-level problem solving analysis and understanding that can help improve the programming skills and competency among its members, and

6. Foster a strong foundation about the fundamentals and essentials of research and development and thereby produce quality researches.
